Why the S&P 500?

  • Indices help as "averages" for determing economic performance.
  • There are ~4266 public companies in the US. (2019)
  • The SP500 accounts for about 12% of all publicly traded companies, but for ~80% of all Market Capitalization.
  • Tracking these companies gives a more representative average than other indices like the Dow Jones Industrial Average, which tracks only 30 US companies.
